### Objective

Develop the Market Heatmap component as shown in the mockup to visualize the relative performance of top cryptocurrencies.

### Tasks

* Create a `MarketHeatmap.tsx` component.
* Design a CSS Grid layout that roughly emulates a treemap, allocating different span sizes to major coins (BTC, ETH, etc.).
* Color-code the boxes (red/green) based on their 24h performance.
* Integrate the component into the right column of `Home.tsx` below the Top Losers.

### Acceptance criteria

* Layout closely resembles the block structure of the mockup.
* Typography inside the blocks is centered, with the symbol prominent and the percentage below it.
* A header with "Market Heatmap" and "View full map" is present.
