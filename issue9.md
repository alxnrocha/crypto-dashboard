### Objective

Create the side panel for Top Gainers, Losers, and trending market data to give users a quick snapshot of market movers.

### Tasks

* Create `MarketHighlights` component.
* Extract and sort the top 3 gainers and losers from the CoinGecko data.
* Display the lists in a compact vertical format.
* Integrate into the right column of the Home dashboard.

### Acceptance criteria

* Renders properly on desktop and adapts to mobile.
* Visually distinguishes positive (green) and negative (red) metrics clearly.
* Correctly calculates the highest and lowest performers from the current dataset.
